What's New in our Foundation?

We have introduced a new way of donating blood for those who will not be able to travel to the nearby Hospitals or Camps.

And here it is! We can reach out to your home anytime to collect your blood and store it to the nearby blood banks for future use. This can be useful for people who are wanting to donate blood but aren't available to travel to the hospitals. Use this opporunity wisely to contribute to the world.

Procedure

You give us a donor request where you can also mention that you want to donate being at home and send us your location at our website. We view your home donation request and arrange our staff to come to your home and collect blood. Once your blood is collected we give it to the nearby Hospital/Camp. If you are unsure about which hospital you're blood is being submitted, No Worries! We send you a message of the details to which blood bank you blood is being sent.
